Sandwiches

Sandwiches are a versatile and convenient meal option, typically made by placing various fillings between two slices of bread. The fillings can range from cold cuts, like ham and turkey, to spreads such as peanut butter or hummus, making sandwiches customizable for any taste or dietary preference. Popular sandwiches include the classic BLT (bacon, lettuce, and tomato), turkey club, and grilled cheese.

Sandwiches can be served cold, like a deli-style sandwich, or hot, like a grilled or toasted sandwich. They are often accompanied by condiments like mustard, mayonnaise, or butter, and can include additional ingredients such as lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, and cheese. The type of bread used can also vary, ranging from white and whole grain to baguettes and ciabatta.

  2. Meat or vegetarian fillings
  3. Cheese (optional)
  4. Condiments
  5. Lettuce
