Hamburgers

Hamburgers are a popular dish consisting of a cooked patty, usually made from ground beef, placed between two halves of a soft bun. The patty is often seasoned with salt, pepper, and additional spices for added flavor. Hamburgers can be customized with a variety of toppings, including cheese, which transforms the dish into a cheeseburger. Other toppings include bacon, avocado, fried eggs, or different types of sauces. Hamburgers are typically grilled, pan-fried, or cooked on a flat-top grill and are a staple in casual dining, fast food, and backyard barbecues. There are also alternative variations such as veggie burgers and turkey burgers for those looking for non-beef options.
